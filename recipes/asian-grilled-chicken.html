Ingredients

Chicken

  • Chicken Breast Fillets- 4 fillets- 400-450 gms

1st Marination:

  • Salt-1 tsp
  • Crushed Pepper- 3/4 tsp
  • Garlic powder- 1/2 tsp

2nd Marinade

  • Sweet Chilli Sauce- 4 tbsp
  • Light Soy Sauce- 2 tsp
  • Lemon Juice- 2 tsp
  • Olive oil- 3 tbsp
  • Red Chilli Flakes- 1 tsp

Preparation

  • Wash and pat dry the chicken fillets. Trim it to make the fillets of equal width and uniform.
  • Now add the salt, crushed pepper and garlic powder. Set aside for 15-20 mins.
  • To prepare the marinade, take a small bowl and add all the items mentioned. Mix and add this to the chicken fillets. Rub it to coat it uniformly.
  • Cover and keep aside for around 1 hour.

Process

  1. Preheat the grill pan and add 1 tbsp olive oil. Brush it evenly over the grill pan & place the chicken pieces side by side.
  2. Grill on one side for 3 mins on medium heat and then flip on the other side. Repeat on the other side for another 3 mins on medium heat.
  3. Now baste with the marinade and grill it on low to medium heat for around 3 mins. Again turn it on the other side and grill it for another 3 mins
  4. Remove and serve with a side of saut√©ed veggies.
