Chicken Gravy

Boneless Chicken Curry Recipe | Quick and Easy Chicken Gravy Recipe | Boneless Chicken Curry | Restaurant Style Chicken Gravy Recipe | Dhaba Style Boneless Chicken Curry | Spice Eats Chicken Curry

Ingredients

Chicken

  • Boneless Chicken - 500 gms (preferably thigh pieces)

Tempering

  • Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  • Black cardamom-2

Paste: (crushed using mortar and pestle)

  • Ginger- 1 inch
  • Garlic cloves- 6
  • Green chillies- 1-2

Spice Powders:

  • Turmeric Powder-1/2 tsp
  • Kashmiri Chilli Powder- 1 tsp
  • Cumin Powder- 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander powder- 1.5 tsp/li>
  • Garam Masala Powder- 1/2 tsp
  • Kasuri Methi, roasted & powdered-1/2 tsp

Other Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes, sliced- 3-4 medium (250 gms red ones)
  • Whisked curd/yoghurt- 4 tbsp
  • Ginger julienned- 2 tbsp (slices)
  • Green Chillies - 2 (cut into 1‚Äù pieces)
  • Chopped coriander leaves- 3 tbsp
  • Salt- 1 tsp
  • Oil-4 tbsp

Preparation

  • Cut the boneless chicken fillets into bite sized pieces.
  • Slice the red tomatoes and cut the green chillies into 1 inch pieces.
  • Also whisk the curd/yoghurt.
  • Chop the coriander leaves and set aside.
  • Dry roast the Kasuri Methi and once cooled, crush it with your hand.

Process

  1. Heat oil in a deep kadhai and add the cumin seeds and black cardamom.
  2. Give a stir and then add the chicken pieces. Fry on high heat for 2-3 mins till the water is dried and the chicken is little browned.
  3. Now add the crushed ginger-garlic-green chilli and salt. Mix and fry on medium heat for 2-3 mins.
  4. Now add all the spice powders other than garam masala powder. Mix and fry on low heat for 2-3 mins.
  5. Now add the sliced tomatoes & green chillies. Also add a pinch of salt.
  6. Fry on medium heat for 2 minutes and then lower heat. Cover & cook on low heat for around 7-8 mins till tomatoes are pulpy.
  7. Now switch off heat and add the whisked curd.
  8. Mix well and then switch on.
  9. Cook on low heat for 2 mins and then add garam masala powder & kasuri methi.
  10. Mix and cover & cook on low heat for 2-3 mins till curd/yogurt is cooked & oil separates.
  11. Now garnish with ginger slice and chopped coriander leaves.
